Frequently Asked Questions

Listing your car is easy! Simply click on "Sell Your Car" in the navigation, create an account or log in, and fill out our step-by-step listing form. You can add photos, set your price, and publish your listing in just minutes.

It's completely free to browse and contact sellers. For sellers, listing your car is free. We only charge a small success fee when your car sells through our platform, which is significantly lower than traditional dealership commissions.

Once you find a car you're interested in, use the "Contact Seller" button on the car listing page to message the seller directly. You can arrange a test drive time and location that works for both parties.

We recommend meeting in public places during daylight hours, bringing a friend or family member with you, verifying the vehicle's VIN matches the listing, and completing transactions at secure locations like banks. Never share personal financial information until you're ready to complete the purchase.
