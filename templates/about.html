About AutoDealer

Your trusted partner in finding and selling quality vehicles since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, AutoDealer began as a small local dealership in Detroit with a simple mission: to make car buying and selling transparent, easy, and trustworthy.

What started as a single location has grown into a nationwide platform connecting thousands of buyers and sellers every month. We've successfully facilitated over 50,000 vehicle transactions.

Today, we're proud to be one of the most trusted names in the automotive marketplace, known for our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the car buying and selling experience by creating a transparent, efficient, and trustworthy marketplace that connects buyers with their perfect vehicles and helps sellers get the best value for their cars.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted automotive marketplace in the country, where anyone can buy or sell a vehicle with complete confidence, supported by innovative technology and exceptional customer service.

Our Core Values

Trust & Transparency

We believe in complete honesty in every transaction, with no hidden fees or surprises.

Customer First

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. Their satisfaction is our top priority.

Quality Assurance

Every vehicle listed meets our rigorous quality standards for safety and reliability.

Integrity

We conduct business with the highest ethical standards, always doing what's right for our customers.
